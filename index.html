About Student Gymkhana

IIIT Vadodara Student-Gymkhana aims at maintaining and providing good extra co-curricular activities to it's students ensuring a perfect balance between their academics & social life through various events conducted by its Clubs headed by different Committees. IIIT Vadodara Student Gymkhana consists of a total 10 Clubs & 4 Committees. All of these Clubs, Committees are quite active in their fields, have brought laurels to the Institute and are growing at a good rate. Members of these Clubs & Committees are always present during their events and are responsible for the growth and development of the Clubs.
